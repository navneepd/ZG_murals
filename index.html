About

Across the heartland of Assam, public walls and concrete canvases bear the indelible mark of devotion to Zubeen Garg. These sites have transcended mere art to become sacred markers of collective memory, forming a widespread pilgrimage of the heart. Fans travel this 'Ballad Trail' to stand where the image of Zubeen Da is preserved, honoring the voice that articulated their joys, struggles, and identity. This outpouring of public reverence transforms these everyday locations into eternal sanctuaries of the Assamese soul, ensuring his spirit remains a living, breathing presence in the land he sang for.
